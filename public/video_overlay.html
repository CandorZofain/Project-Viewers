Winner

Rules

Main Goal

Attack and destroy the enemy base.

Controls

Click on hexagons to show a list of actions.

Moving

Players can move to any adjacent hexagon, unless the opposing team is occupying it.

Defending

Players that are defending take reduced damage in battles.

Gathering

Players on crystal spaces can collect 10 crystals. Players take increased damage while gathering.

Repairing

Players at their base can spend 50 crystals to add 10 HP to the base. Players take increased damage while repairing.

Attacking

Players can attack the opposing team by trying to move onto a space the opposing team occupies or when both teams try to move onto the same space.

Elements

Each player has an element which affects battles. Players can have a neutral, fire, water, wind, or earth element. To change elements, players must be at their base and must have at least 1 crystal. Changing elements does NOT end your turn.

Neutral element deals reduced damage to enemy players and the enemy base.

Fire is strong against wind.
Water is strong against fire.
Earth is strong against water.
Wind is strong against earth.
